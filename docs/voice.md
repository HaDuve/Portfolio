# Voice & Copy Rules — hannesduve.com

How the Site speaks. Pair with `CONTEXT.md` (what terms mean) and `docs/design-system.md` (how it looks).

## Register

- **`du` everywhere**, both locales' DE copy and both audiences (Freelance + Coaching). No `Sie`. No mid-page register switch.
- EN: plain, direct second person ("you"). Same warmth, no hype.

## Positioning axis

One philosophy across both offerings: **durable, maintainable software you can rely on.**

- **Freelance Lane / landing:** German-engineering register — **hohe Qualität, stabil, verlässlich, wartbar**. Senior-level, production-grade. Do **not** frame as "anti–vibe coding"; do not disparage AI coding.
- **Coaching Lane / landing:** **Vibe Coding** owned positively — build with AI *and* keep it maintainable. Same quality value, taught.

## Terminology

- Coaching activity: **"Vibe Coding"** as the hook + **"Programmieren mit KI" / "Coding with AI"** as the plain explainer. Don't use "vibe coding" negatively anywhere.
- Tools: **Cursor** (lead) + **Claude** (underlying AI).

## Banned / rejected wording

- ❌ **"weggeworfen"** (throwaway framing)
- ❌ **"trägt" / "tragen"** — incl. the old hero headline *"Apps und Web, die tragen."* (needs replacement)
- ❌ Generic marketing filler; framework-default platitudes.

## Tone

- Concrete, senior, calm. Show substance (real projects, rates, process) over adjectives.
- Tight sentences. One idea per line in cards/strips.

## Hero

- **DE headline:** "Software, die hält, was sie verspricht." (quality + reliability; replaces the rejected "die tragen").
- **DE subhead:** "Ich baue stabile Apps, Websites und Server für dein Unternehmen — oder zeige dir 1:1, wie du selbst mit Cursor und Claude baust. Hohe Qualität, direkt und persönlich."
- Keep dual CTA: Projekt anfragen · Coaching buchen.

## Plain German over jargon

- Prefer plain German in customer-facing copy: **"Websites"** (not "Web"), **"Server"** (not "Backend"), where natural. Tech tags/stack lists may keep technical terms.
- Differentiator phrasing: **"Hohe Qualität, direkt und persönlich"** rather than "Senior-Level / ohne Agentur-Overhead".
